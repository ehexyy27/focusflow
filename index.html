Classic 25/5 rhythm out of the box. Or invent your own — 52 on, 17 off, 90-minute deep blocks, whatever matches your brain on a given day. The instrument bends to you, not the other way around.

Focus analytics.

Daily, weekly, monthly. Discover when your brain actually shows up, then protect those hours like an animal.
